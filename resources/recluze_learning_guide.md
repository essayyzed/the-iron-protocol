## ðŸŽ¯ Key Takeaways from Recluze's Philosophy

1. **Learn slowly and deliberately** - 10 years to mastery, not 10 weeks
2. **Master basics before using AI** - ChatGPT/Copilot are tools, not crutches
3. **Problem-solving > memorization** - Understand patterns, not solutions
4. **Build from first principles** - Don't just use libraries, understand them
5. **Website > LinkedIn** - Portfolio matters more than profile
6. **Don't specialize too early** - Keep doors open in early years
7. **Central concepts matter** - Remember the core, details will follow
8. **Attention span is key** - Avoid shorts, reels, and surface-level content
9. **LeetCode isn't for beginners** - Learn proper programming first
10. **Take first job offer** - Fresh grads: get experience, negotiate later
